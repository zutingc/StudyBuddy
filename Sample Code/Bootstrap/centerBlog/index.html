And we’re back! The holidays, right?

2016 was a loaded year in every sense of the word, but in the end it was one of our best to date. Join us below for a look back at our favorite moments, projects, and stories of 2016.



When taking stock of 2016, we couldn't believe how much took place since last January. In the past 12 months we've done some of our best work. We've learned and grown as a company, and we've seen some wonderful people come and go. Join us for a brief look back at 2016...



Launched this summer, the Grovemade Pocket Knife was new territory for us. Our first real “tool”, the final product had to be safe, extremely precise, and beautiful, god willing. The design process was a fitting journey.



In August, we welcomed Nomad Film Co, a group of young filmmakers from Southern California, into our shop. Like us, they were on the hunt for inspiration, driving up the coast and talking to like-minded creatives. As a perfect compliment, we invited our good friend, Portland photographer William Anthony, to come tell his story and our own.



Also in August, we launched the Grovemade Wood Speaker, marking the first time we’ve collaborated with industrial designer Joey Roth. Read the full story on how we designed the system together.



We ran a contest back in October to find the best desk setups and those in need of an overhaul. Grovemade CEO & Co-founder Ken Tomita really got into the spirit of things and decided to not only reset his desk but to build a new one entirely. Follow his progress...

We also put together a step-by-step guide to walk you through building your own simple standing desk. Adult supervision required!
